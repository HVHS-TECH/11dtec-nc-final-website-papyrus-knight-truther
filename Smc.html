Shadow Milk Cookie is a character from Cookie Run Kingdom. He is one of the five beast cookies. He used to be the fount of knowlege but his virtue has crumbled and he is now the cookie of deciet.

Shadow milk cookie is one of my favourite cookie run characters for many reasons. Firstly, he has such a big ego but he is written in the way where he is a lovable jerk rather than a genuinley annoying character. Second, his character design is really pleaseing to look at, (plus he is a jester, the best kind of character.) Also, his voice actor CJ Pawlikowski, really helps bring the character to life. He gives shadow milk a very energetic voice. often with very high inflections, which really helps lean into the jester aspect of the character. And when needed, he drops alot of the bounciness and goes for a more "tantrum-y" voice, which shows just how immature Shadow Milk can be sometimes.
