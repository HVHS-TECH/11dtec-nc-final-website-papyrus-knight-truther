My top 5!

Shadow Milk Cookie is a character from Cookie Run Kingdom. He is one of the five beast cookies. He used to be the fount of knowlege but his virtue has crumbled and he is now the cookie of deciet.

Shadow milk cookie is one of my favourite cookie run characters for many reasons. Firstly, he is a trickster that loves the idea of spreading deciet. he is very theatrical and egotistical. However, in his Beast Yeast episode Pure Vanilla Cookie tells him that he can tell how lonely he truly is and offers to be his friend. Which Shadow Milk does not take well (my man got friend zoned loll). Also, his voice actor CJ Pawlikowski, really helps bring the character to life. He gives shadow milk a very energetic voice. often with very high inflections, which really helps lean into the theatrical aspect of the character. And when needed, he drops alot of the bounciness and goes for a more angry (yet still screechy) voice, which I feel really adds to the character.

Lastly, his character design is really pleaseing to look at, (plus he is a jester, the best kind of character.) His character design consists of lots of deep rich blue, which I find very nice to look at. His hair looks like a jester hat, fitting with his overall jester theming. Also in his hair are lots of eyes, which are a motif for Shadow milk. And all of the highlights make his hair look super shiny which I really like.
