My Top 5!

Toby Fox is one of the most reveloutionary figures in game development. From his music to his writing, he has changed how people perceive video games forever. Hhe started just as the man behind the halloween hack for earthbound and climbed his way up to being the creator behind two of the most popular indie games in the world. In fact, the release of the latest chapters of DELTARUNE actually crashed valve with how many people scrambled to buy it on release day.

Something that alot of Toby Fox fans can agree apon is that UNDERTALE comes to you when you need it most. I whole-heartedly believe this, whether your life is far too busy and you need some down time. Or you simply want a new game to play. Or whatever it may be, I highly reccomend playing.

Even if you haven't played his games, i guarentee you have atleast seen, or heard some of Toby's work before. His music appears in countless youtube videos, his characters are spread all over the internet, and there are even references to his work in newer pokemon games.

From here on out, this site will contain spoilers for both UNDERTALE and DELTARUNE. read at your own risk.
