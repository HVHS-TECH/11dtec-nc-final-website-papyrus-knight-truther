My top 5!

Undyne is probably one of my favourite UNDERTALE characters. She is the head of the royal guard, and one of the main antagonist that the player has to face before she changes to your side.

Undyne is very lively and hot headed. She takes everything as a challenge and refuses to be defeated. She also can be heard playing Alphys's theme on piano before you enter her house with papyrus after her boss fight (down bad lesbian.) We love a strong lesbian character.

Also her design is super sick. She is a blue fish monster with red hair. she only has one eye and wears an eye patch, which makes her look super cool. Undyne can also be seen wearing a full suit of armour and using magical spears as her weapon. ALso super cool. Undyne is just one of the coolest characters ever.
