My top 5!

Spamton is DELTARUNE chapter 2's secret boss, and when defeated he gives the player a shadow crystal, and himself as an inventory item. the item is different depending on how you defeat him.

Spamton is an unhinged salesman, who interupts the player when they go through a back alley. (He also helps with the weird route but thats not important right now.) His speech is constantly interupted with ad's and he also has very heavy connections to Gaster, Friend, and Tenna.

His character designs are also really cool. With his normal design, he is a short character with pink and yellow glasses, a long nose, slicked back hair, and a black suit. His NEO design keeps his head, but plasters it onto a large robot body held up with strings that has large wings and a cannon for his right arm. (boy that sounds farmilliar...)
