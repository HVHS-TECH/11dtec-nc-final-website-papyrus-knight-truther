Affogato Cookie is a character from Cookie Run Kingdom. He used to work closely under Dark Cacao Cookie before attempting to overthrow him and take over the kingdom.

Affogato Cookie acts stuck up and better than most people. He wants to live lavishly, and share that lifestyle with the rest of the Dark Cacao Kingdom. He is a manipulative and cunning character. he is patient and slowly undermines the citezens trust in their king, making it easier to overthrow the kingdom. he easily twists peoples words, making it easy to shift the blame.

Lastly, his character design is gorgeous. His character design consists of lots of purple, my favourite colour Affogato Cookie dresses in long flowy robes, with a fluffy... thing (I'm not sure what that is)
He has a beautiful hairpin and staff. This extravagant character design only further exagerates his lust for a lavish lifestyle, despite how life is in the Dark Cacao Kingdom.
