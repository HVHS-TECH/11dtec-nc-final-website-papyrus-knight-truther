My top 5!

Everyone knows about fictional characters, whether from a book, movie, TV show, or anything else, these characters bring joy to many people all over the world. And of course everyone has their favourite characters. they could be your favourite because you relate to them, maybe they have an interesting role in the story, or maybe they just have a cool design. No matter the reason, we can't deny that fictional characters play a huge role in many peoples lives.

I love so many characters from a whole bunch of different pieces of media, and if I went over them all I think my computer may explode, so I'll stick to just a few of my favourites. I'll go over the main traits of each character and why they are my favourites.

From here on out this site will contain spoilers for Cookie run kingdom, UNDERTALE, DELTARUNE, and Gachiakuta. read at ur own risk. Please keep in mind that all characters shown are just my opinion! Always be kind in fandom spaces and respect the creators!
