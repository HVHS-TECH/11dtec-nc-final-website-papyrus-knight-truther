August Stilza is a character from Gachiakuta. he makes the outfits, and full face masks for the cleaners. I havent finished reading the manga, and have only seen the anime so take what I say here with a grain of salt.

August is a very loud character. He is very blunt and doesn't seem to understand privacy. The cleaners even say that they cant give him a time limit or he produces something unwearable. He also rides a motorcycle. So in other words, he is awesome and the best character in gachiakuta.

He has long blonde hair with orange glasses and headphones, and he wears blue overalls with whack looking shoes.
