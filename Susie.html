Susie is a character from DELTARUNE. she plays the role as one of the main characters in your party.

Susie is a lively and loud character. she starts off as a bully who will destroy anything in her path, but over the course of the current chapters we have she becomes more hopefull and willing to go along with the rest of the fun gang. She loves violence and things that look cool. She is probably my favourite character in DELTARUNE.

Susies characters design is absolutley awesome. She appears to be some kind of purple dinosaur with dark brown hair. In the light world she sports some blue jeans, a white shirt and a purple jacket. In the dark world however, her design becomes much more vibrant. Her colour pallate changes to a more pink and vibrant pallate, and her clothes become ten times cooler, she sports a sick vest with a belt, and she has spiked arm bands. She also has a giant axe in battle, making her immediatley more awesome.
