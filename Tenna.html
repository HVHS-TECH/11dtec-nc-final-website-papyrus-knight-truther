Tenna is the main antagonist of chapter 3 of DELTARUNE. He is a loud TV host and also Kris's TV.

He is quite... Bipolar. He becomes very sad and small when susie tells him that they arent gonna play his game show and he guilt trips them into playing. and when it works he immediatley becomes huge and happy and very energetic. his speech is similar to spamtons, exept instead of being interupted by ads, its enchanced with word art and music that helps him exagerate his wackiness.

His character design is quite striking. A bright red suit with yellow accents and a TV for his head with a long nose.
