My top 5!

Mettaton is a character from UNDERTALE by Toby Fox, he is a robot with a soul made by Alphys who was turned into a TV star.

The first reason I absolutley ADORE this guy, is because of his confident, theatrical, and charismatic personality. Mettatons personality is, (like I stated before) quite theatrical. He loves drama, action, and fame. Most of his on screen time, he is only acting as a killer robot to help Alphys appear as a hero to the player.
However, nearing the end of the core, Mettaton reveals his true intentions and his dreams to entertain the entirety of humanity. He tells the player that he want's to leave the underground to entertain even if it means taking the humans SOUL. Starting his main boss fight and the player flips a switch on his back turning him into Mettaton EX.

Also, his character designs are amazing.
Mettaton's main design is a plain box with a screen, some hands, and a singular wheel. However his EX and NEO designs are much better in my opinion. Mettaton's EX design is more humanoid, with black hair that covers his right eye. He has a pink chestplate and boots, with black shoulderpads. And in the center of his torso, is what I think contains his soul. I don't believe it is his soul, as monster souls are upside down hearts and white rather than a pink right side up heart, more like the human souls. Mettaton's NEO design is similar to his EX design, exept his hair is flipped up revealing his other eye, he has giant wings, his right arm becoms a cannon, and his overall design becomes a lot more pointy.
