Pure Vanilla Cookie is a character from Cookie Run Kingdom. He is one of the five anchient cookies. He used to be the ruler of the Vanilla Kingdom.

Pure Vanilla Cookie is on my list of favourite Characters for many reasons.
Reason number one is his personality. Pure Vanilla Cookie is unrealistically kind. He gives his counterpart, Shadow Milk Cookie more chances than he deserves. However, his kindness starts to be too selfless, as he sacrifices himself to save his friends by letting deceit take over and briefly becoming Truthless Recluse before gaining his awakened form. Similar to Shadow Milk, Pure Vanilla's voice actor really helps bring the character to life, giving him a soft voice filled with kindness.

Lastly, his character design is really nice. He has very muted yellow and beige colours in his design, and a tall pointed hat. Also, he has a long flowing cloak with ribbons that look like wafers. In his awakened form he also has long flowing hair.
